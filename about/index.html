Our Background

Pinnacle Technologies Consultants Ltd is an engineering and technical services company established in March 2024 in Rwanda.

The company was founded with a mission to deliver efficient, safe, and sustainable engineering solutions tailored to modern infrastructure and energy needs.

We operate across multiple sectors, including electrical engineering, industrial automation, construction, and power systems, supporting both private and public sector clients.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality engineering solutions that enhance efficiency, reliability, and sustainability.

Our Vision

To become a leading engineering solutions provider in Africa, driving innovation in energy and infrastructure development.
